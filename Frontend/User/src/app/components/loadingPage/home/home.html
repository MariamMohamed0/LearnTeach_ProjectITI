How it Works

Discover our comprehensive learning platform policies.

1

Platform Concept

Our platform is a skill-exchange learning system where users teach and learn from each other through a fair and balanced points mechanism.

2

Point Earning System

The website operates on a barter-based model: every user is both a learner and a teacher, exchanging knowledge instead of money.

3

Point Spending System

Users earn points when they teach others, encouraging active contribution and knowledge sharing within the community.

4

Penalty for Non-Teaching

If a user attends a session only as a learner without providing any teaching sessions to others, they do not earn points.

5

Fair Participation Rule

To maintain balance, 20 points are deducted from learners who attend sessions without teaching in return.

6

Community Learning

This system ensures fairness, promotes active engagement, and motivates users to share their expertise instead of only consuming knowledge.

7

Reward-Contribution

Teach to earn. Learn responsibly. The more you contribute, the more opportunities you unlock.

8

Progress Tracking

Your growth is tracked through badges and ratings
